## Design Context

### Users
Users seeking a safe, non-judgmental space for photo sharing and light social discovery. They want to express and react to the "mood" and "vibe" of moments, rather than participating in appearance ranking or performance-driven social media.

### Brand Personality
Cute but not childish, dreamy but readable, soft, collectible, and gentle. The core identity is a "pastel pixel world where people leave cat paw stamps on the mood of photos and collect taste like jelly."

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone:** Pastel pixel aesthetic.
- **Colors:** Soft lavender, baby pink, sky blue, cream white (dominant for airy spacing), and soft mint as a limited accent.
- **Theme:** Primarily light/cream-based to support the soft pastel palette.
- **Typography:** Soft Korean-friendly UI typography paired with pixel-style display treatments for headings/badges. Must remain highly legible.
- **Anti-references:** Generic SaaS UI, black/white luxury minimal clones, heavy gradients, card-on-card clutter, Instagram story-circle clones, and anything resembling a 5-star rating or "heart" based system.

### Design Principles
1. **Mood over Metrics:** All interactions must reinforce reacting to the atmosphere/taste, explicitly avoiding appearance scoring.
2. **Distinctive "Stamping" Interaction:** Primary reactions use a 1-5 cat paw "stamp" system (슬쩍, 콕, 꾹, 폭닥, 젤리) instead of generic likes.
3. **Photo-First & Airy:** Content is the hero. Use edge-to-edge photos and generous spacing to avoid visual clutter.
4. **Collectible Worldbuilding:** UI elements like the "Chalna" (disappearing posts) strip should feel like collecting stamps or polaroids, not generic social media patterns.
5. **Safe & Gentle:** The UI, copy, and flows must feel inherently safe, encouraging positive, mood-oriented engagement over toxic or competitive behavior.
